### Week 2: The Core Logic (Document-to-Quiz)

* **Goal:** Build a basic document-to-quiz chatbot.
* **Action:** Connect your extracted text to an LLM, like GPT-3.5 or a local MPT-7B.
* **Action:** Prompt the LLM to generate intelligent questions from the educational content.
* **Action:** Program basic question difficulty levels, such as easy, medium, and hard.
